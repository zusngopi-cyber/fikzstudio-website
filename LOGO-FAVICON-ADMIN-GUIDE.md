# Logo & Favicon Upload - Admin Panel ✅

## What's New?

You can now upload your logo and favicon directly from the admin panel! No need to manually edit code or place files in folders.

---

## How to Use

### Step 1: Go to Settings
1. Login to admin panel: `/admin`
2. Click on **"Settings"** in the sidebar
3. Scroll to the **"Logo & Favicon"** section (at the top)

### Step 2: Upload Your Logo
1. Click **"Choose File"** under "Site Logo"
2. Select your logo image (PNG, JPG, or SVG)
3. Click **"Open"**
4. ✅ Your logo is uploaded instantly!

### Step 3: Upload Your Favicon
1. Click **"Choose File"** under "Favicon"
2. Select your favicon image (square image recommended)
3. Click **"Open"**
4. ✅ Your favicon is uploaded instantly!

### Step 4: See Changes
1. **Refresh the page** (F5)
2. Your logo appears in the navigation bar
3. Your favicon appears in the browser tab

- **Formats:** PNG, JPG, SVG, GIF
- **Recommended Size:** At least 200x200px

- **Remove:** Click "Remove" to delete uploaded favicon
- **Live Update:** Favicon changes immediately in browser tab

### ✅ Automatic Integration
- Logo automatically appears in navigation
- Favicon automatically appears in browser tab
- No code editing required
- No file management needed

## Image Requirements

### Logo:
```
✅ Good:
- PNG with transparent background
- SVG vector file
- High resolution (at least 200x200px)
- Clear and readable

❌ Avoid:
- Very low resolution
- Blurry images
- Images with text that's too small
```

### Favicon:
```
✅ Good:

- Simple design (recognizable at small size)
- High contrast
- 32x32px or larger

❌ Avoid:
- Rectangular images
- Too much detail
- Very low resolution
```

## How It Works

### Storage:
- Images are stored in your browser's localStorage
- Converted to base64 format
- No server upload needed
- Works offline

### Display:
- Navigation component checks for uploaded logo
- If logo exists, displays it
- If no logo, shows default "F" icon
- Favicon updates in real-time

## Troubleshooting

### Logo Not Showing?
1. **Refresh the page** (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. **Clear cache** (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
3. **Check image format** (PNG, JPG, SVG only)
4. **Try smaller file** (under 2MB recommended)

### Favicon Not Showing?
1. **Hard refresh** (Ctrl+Shift+R)
2. **Close and reopen browser**
3. **Check image is square**
4. **Try different format** (PNG recommended)

### Image Too Big/Small?
**Logo:**
- Image will auto-scale to 40px height
- Width adjusts automatically
- Use high-resolution for best quality

**Favicon:**
- Displays at 16x16px or 32x32px
- Upload at least 32x32px
- Larger is better (up to 512x512px)

### Want to Change Logo?
1. Go to Settings
2. Click "Remove" under current logo
3. Upload new logo
4. Refresh page

## Tips for Best Results

### Logo Tips:
1. **Use transparent background** (PNG format)
2. **Keep it simple** - should be readable at small size
3. **High resolution** - at least 200x200px
4. **Test on mobile** - check how it looks on small screens

### Favicon Tips:
1. **Keep it simple** - should be recognizable at 16x16px
2. **Use high contrast** - stands out in browser tab
3. **Square format** - 1:1 aspect ratio
4. **Test in browser** - see how it looks in tab

- Stored in browser localStorage
- Larger files = slower loading
- Optimize images before upload
