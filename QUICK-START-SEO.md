### Day 2: Get Your First Reviews

**Time Required:** 1 hour

1. **Identify 5 Happy Clients**
   - Choose clients you've done great work for
   - Make sure they're satisfied with your service

2. **Ask for Reviews (Template)**
   ```
   Hi [Client Name],

   I hope you're happy with the website we created for you! 
   
   Would you mind leaving us a quick review on Google? It would 
   really help other businesses find us.
   
   Here's the link: [Your Google Review Link]
   
   Thank you so much!
   
   Best regards,
   Fikzstudio
   ```

3. **Make It Easy**
   - Send them the direct review link
   - Follow up if they don't respond in 3 days
   - Thank them when they leave a review

4. **Respond to All Reviews**
   - Reply within 24 hours
   - Thank them for positive reviews
   - Address concerns in negative reviews professionally

**Goal:** Get 3-5 reviews in the first week
