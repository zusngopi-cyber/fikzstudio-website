## üí° Pro Tips

### For Faster Rankings
1. **Focus on long-tail keywords first** - Less competition, faster results
2. **Create location-specific pages** - Separate pages for each service area
3. **Get reviews on Google** - Ask satisfied clients for reviews
4. **Build citations consistently** - Same NAP (Name, Address, Phone) everywhere
5. **Create video content** - YouTube videos rank well in Google

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ‚ùå Keyword stuffing
- ‚ùå Buying backlinks
- ‚ùå Duplicate content
- ‚ùå Ignoring mobile optimization
